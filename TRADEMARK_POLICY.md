# LibreFit Brand Guidelines

## Purpose of These Guidelines

The project's code is open source to encourage collaboration. The brand—specifically the name "
LibreFit" and its logos—serves as the official identifier for the project and a symbol of its
quality and reputation.

These guidelines exist to ensure that the brand is used consistently and to prevent confusion among
users. It is important that users can be confident that anything bearing the official brand is the
official work.

## Usage Rules

| ✅ Permitted Use                                                                   | ❌ Restricted Use                                                        |
|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Use the LibreFit name and logo to refer to the **official** project.              | Use the LibreFit name or logo for your **own fork** of the project.     |
| Use the logo in articles, reviews, or tutorials about the official project.       | Imply that your project is **endorsed by or affiliated with** LibreFit. |
| State that your software is **"for LibreFit"** or **"compatible with LibreFit"**. | Use "LibreFit" as part of your own project, company, or domain name.    |
